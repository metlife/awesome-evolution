# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This repository is a community space for sharing and improving Markdown-based resources about evolution.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and collaborative.
- Assume good intent and communicate with kindness.
- Give helpful feedback focused on content quality.
- Cite sources where appropriate and avoid misinformation.
- Be open to correction and willing to update content.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech, slurs, or demeaning language.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Spam, self-promotion, or off-topic disruptive content.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all participation in this repository, including issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and comments.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by contacting the maintainers directly. For sensitive reports, prefer a private channel (e.g., email/contact method listed in the repository); for non-sensitive reports, you may open an issue.

Include as much relevant context as possible (links, screenshots, timestamps) so maintainers can review quickly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take any action they deem appropriate, including removing content, issuing warnings, temporarily restricting participation, or banning users for severe or repeated violations.

## Good-Faith Participation

We value thoughtful disagreement and evidence-based discussion. Differences in scientific interpretation are expected, but disrespectful behavior is not.
